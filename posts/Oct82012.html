Repulsion - October 8, 2012

There are of course things

 I wish I had said.

Things about cold rainwater

And thoughts you cannot do without.

Thinking that if I had shared my veiw

You would have turned away.

It has always been my thought

That your lack of enthusiasm

Was not a symtom of anything in you

But a silent reaction to a repulsion from me

A force I cannot see

And which I never even thought

I could control.
